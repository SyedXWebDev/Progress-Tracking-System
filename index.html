Daily Progress Documentation System

Hey, this is my little digital progress tracker for leveling up through self-directed learning and personal growth. I built this to keep myself accountable and to make consistency my superpower by tracking my progress every day.

I'm starting to see how much tracking my progress lights a fire under me. So, I'm going all-in with some visual ways to record what I do each day. This is where I'll dump all my daily wins, tasks, and random thoughts. Just me, showing up for myself!

The Beginning



Alright, so this is where it all kicked off — May 21, 2024. I started snapping daily headshots, just to capture memories and maybe spot changes in my face over time. I was just tossing them into my Google Drive, nothing fancy. A few months in, I realized — whoa, I've unintentionally documented this whole transformation of my facial features. It's like a time-lapse of me! That got me thinking: why not do this for "everything" in my life? Tracking progress like this feels like a reward for showing up every day. It's my way of celebrating consistency, and I'm hyped to keep it going!
